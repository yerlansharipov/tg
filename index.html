WHO WE ARE

We are a dance community in the heart of Almaty dedicated to those who dreamed of dancing but were afraid to start.

At TOP Generation, we specialize in Hip-Hop, Urban Dance, and street styles, creating a welcoming space where beginners can discover their passion for movement without judgment.

Every dancer started from zero. Your journey begins here.

WHAT WE TEACH

Hip-Hop Beginner

Start from zero. Learn the foundations of hip-hop in a supportive environment. Build confidence with every move.

Hip-Hop Intermediate

Level up your skills. Complex choreography and personal style development. For dancers ready to grow.

Urban Dance

Contemporary street styles. Popping, locking, house, and more. Expand your dance vocabulary.

Choreography

Learn complete routines to popular music. Performance-ready skills for showcases and competitions.

Kids & Teens

Hip-hop fundamentals for young dancers. Build confidence, creativity, and community.

Open Training

Practice your moves. Work on choreography. Dance in a supportive space. All levels welcome.
